1 Initialization

set.seed(1)
x1 <- runif(100)
x2 <- 0.5 * x1 + rnorm(100) / 10
y <- 2 + 2 * x1 + 0.3 * x2 + rnorm(100)

Linear model: \(y = 2 + 2x_1 + 0.3x_2\) with \(\beta_0=2, \beta_1=2,\) and \(\beta_2=0.3\).

2 Correlation

cor(x1, x2)
## [1] 0.8351212
library(ggplot2)
qplot(x=x1, y=x2)

\(x_1\) and \(x_2\) have high positive correlation.
